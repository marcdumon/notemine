# Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra Review: The Best Android Phone of 2025

Samsung's Galaxy S25 Ultra arrives with a Snapdragon 8 Elite chip, a redesigned titanium frame, and a 200 MP main camera. At €1,349 for the base 256 GB model, it is Samsung's most expensive phone yet — but does it justify the price?

## Performance

The Snapdragon 8 Elite, developed by Qualcomm and manufactured by TSMC on a 3nm process, delivers a 30% generational leap in performance over the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3. In Geekbench 6 multi-core tests, the S25 Ultra scores 7,840 — comfortably ahead of the Apple iPhone 16 Pro Max (7,210).

## Camera

The 200 MP ISOCELL HP9 sensor, combined with Samsung's ProVisual Engine AI processing, produces outstanding results in daylight. Low-light performance lags slightly behind the iPhone 16 Pro Max and Google Pixel 9 Pro, particularly for video.

## Battery and AI

The 5,000 mAh battery easily lasts a full day under heavy use. Samsung Galaxy AI features — including Live Translate, Note Assist, and Circle to Search powered by Google Gemini — are genuinely useful, unlike many AI additions from competitors.

## Verdict

If you are in the Samsung ecosystem, the Galaxy S25 Ultra is an easy recommendation. Its only real competitor is the iPhone 16 Pro Max, which edges it out in video quality but not in versatility.

**Score: 9/10**
